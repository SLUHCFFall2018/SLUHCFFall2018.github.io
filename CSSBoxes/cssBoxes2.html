There are 3 properties that control how a box appears in terms of its relation to its content.

  1. border - seperates the edge of one box from another
  2. padding - is the space between the border of a box and any content contained within it
  3. margin - sit outside of the border, used to create a gap between the two borders of two adjacent boxes
