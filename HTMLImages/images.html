Below are some examples of how to use the HTML image tag



Below is an example of a picture followed by a block-level element, a paragraph. Block-level elements always appear on a new line.

Fruit is a delicious food. It is sweet and has many vitamins and minerals. There are many different types of fruit. Only a few of the many types fruit are available to us in the United States. Many other types of fruit are available in different countries throughout the world. These fruits have flavors unfamiliar to us but after trying them a couple times it is easy to get use to the flavor.
